Hackathon Project Name

The New Revolution with no forgotten passwords

This web browser is good for people of all ages. This web browser helps you keep your information safe from hackers worldwide.




Security

You don't need to use any passwords anymore. This web browser allows you to use face recognition to keep your information safe from others that you don't want going through your private information



Dark Mode

This gives less strain to the eyes along with saving battery on every device. It is also prefered by most developers.



Our Plans for The Future

We plan on making a desktop version that allows you to both login and logout of your windows account with your face recognition. We plan on making touch ID for more security. This will give us a chance to help everyone keep their information safe from hackers.
